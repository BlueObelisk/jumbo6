Papers

Overview

This is a record of as many (peer-reviewed) publications on CML and JUMBO that we can manage. We have attempted to adhere to the copyright restrictions of publishers (even though we do not find these helpful). Remember that some of the earlier papers describe CML syntax that has since been obsoleted. We have published a large series of papers on CML, mainly in the Journal of Chemical Information and Modeling. This is a closed access journal so we cannot reproduce the final version, but we have included pre-review drafts of the papers which may give useful information

Many papers on CML and JUMBO have been published as part of the regular UK eScience all-hands meeting and as these are Open Access are included here.
